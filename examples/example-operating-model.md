# Example Operating Model

This example demonstrates how an organization may structure leadership, governance, and execution.

---

## Strategic Objectives

- expand digital capabilities
- improve operational efficiency
- accelerate product delivery

---

## Governance Structure

Executive Steering Committee  
Provides strategic oversight.

Program Governance Council  
Reviews program progress and resolves cross-team issues.

---

## Execution Structure

Programs coordinate work across engineering, operations, and product teams.

Delivery teams implement initiatives under program leadership.
